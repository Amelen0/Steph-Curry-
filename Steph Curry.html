Stephen Curry

The Splash Bro.

  • 1988 - Born on March 14, 1988 to Dell and Sonya Curry.
  • 2006 - Walked on to the Davidson College basketball team as a 160 pound freshman.
  • 2007 - Led the Davidson Wildcats to an championship game averaging 25.5 ppg, 2.8 assists, and 4.5 Rebounds per game.
  • 2009 - Selected as the 7th overall pick in the 2009 nba draft by the Golden State Warriors.
  • 2011 - Won the nba skills challenge.
  • 2012 - Rose to stardom by hitting a team high of 11 three point shots in a game as well as scoring 54 points in a game.
  • 2014 - Led his team to the nba championship game and earned his first MVP award.
  • 2016 History in the making with his team going 26-0 and stomping their way to a back to back potential finals appeareance.

"Being a superstar means you’ve reached your potential, and I don’t think I’ve reached my potential as a basketball player and as a leader yet."
